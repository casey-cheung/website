I’m a PhD candidate in Condensed Matter Physics at the University of Manchester, working in the Two-dimensional (2D) Materials Nanofabrication Group at the National Graphene Institute. My research explores the mechanical and tribological properties of 2D materials with novel experimental techniques, with a focus on how interlayer interactions govern their performance in nanoscale devices.

Prior to joining the current group, I completed my BSc in Physics at the University of Hong Kong and MSc in Nanomaterials at the University of Manchester.
